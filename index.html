Herbal Restorative Broth

Introducing our flagship Restorative Broths. We’ve meticulously crafted a 100% vegetarian, functionally potent ritual that mimics the deep nourishment and umami mouthfeel of traditional bone broths.

Utilizing premium freeze-dried ingredients like Moringa, Ashwagandha, and Shiitake, this shelf-stable kit provides immediate cognitive clarity and joint lubrication. Just add hot water and ghee.

What Does It Stand For?

It stands for the "Purposeful Pause." In a world where everyone is overworked, over-screened, and under-nourished, this broth stands for putting real, ancient healing back into the modern daily routine without adding friction to a busy day.

What Is It?

It is a 100% vegetarian, instant healing soup. Traditional "bone broths" are famous for repairing joints and gut health, but they require boiling meat for 24 hours. We have recreated that exact same thick, comforting texture and deep nutritional benefit using purely plant-based Ayurvedic superfoods—like freeze-dried Moringa (drumstick), Ashwagandha, and Shiitake mushrooms. You just add hot water, steep for 3 minutes, add a spoon of ghee, and it's ready.

How Does It Impact People's Lives?

It actively repairs the body rather than just stimulating it. When people hit that 4:00 PM afternoon slump, they usually reach for coffee or sugary chai. That spikes cortisol (stress) and leads to a crash. This broth does the exact opposite: it calms the nervous system, clears brain fog, lubricates dry/stiff joints, and restores deep cellular energy (Ojas). It turns a stressful afternoon into a grounding, nourishing ritual.

Why Do We Have To Use It?

Because modern fatigue is a nutritional crisis, not just a sleep crisis. Coffee only masks exhaustion; it doesn't fix the underlying depletion. We have to use this because our bodies need a daily, accessible way to repair tissue, soothe the gut, and calm the mind in the middle of the workday.

  • Zero Friction: Ready in 3 minutes. No cooking required.
